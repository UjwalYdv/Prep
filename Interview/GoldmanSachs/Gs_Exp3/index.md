# 💼 Interview Experience – Goldman Sachs Summer Analyst Intern

**Type:** On-Campus Opportunity  
**Role:** Summer Analyst Intern  
**Total Rounds:** 4 (Online Assessment + 3 Technical Interviews)

---

## 💻 Round 1 – Online Assessment

The first round consisted of **3 DSA questions** and **MCQs** from OOPs and Aptitude.

- One of the coding questions was:
  - **Robot in Grid Problem** → [Unique Paths | LeetCode #62](https://leetcode.com/problems/unique-paths/)  
    _(Determine if a robot can reach the bottom-right corner starting from (0,0) with movement constraints.)_
- Other DSA questions were not remembered by the author.
- The **MCQs** focused on **Object-Oriented Programming** concepts and **basic aptitude**.

✅ **Outcome:** Shortlisted for Technical Interviews

---

## ⚙️ Round 2 – Technical Interview 1

This round began with a discussion on **subjects studied till the 4th semester**, followed by **in-depth project discussion**.  
The interviewer asked several **conceptual and DSA-related questions**, including:

- Difference between **C and C++**
- Concepts of **OOPs**
- One DSA question:
  - **Topological Sort** → [Topological Sorting | GeeksforGeeks](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/topological-sorting/)
- Follow-up questions:
  - **Implement Stack using Queue** → [Implement Stack using Queues | LeetCode #225](https://leetcode.com/problems/implement-stack-using-queues/)
  - **Implement Queue using Stack** → [Implement Queue using Stacks | LeetCode #232](https://leetcode.com/problems/implement-queue-using-stacks/)

✅ **Outcome:** Shortlisted for next round

---

## ⚙️ Round 3 – Technical Interview 2

This round included **3 DSA and puzzle questions** with a focus on **logic, optimization, and problem-solving clarity**.

1. **Check if two nodes belong to the same tree** (given `left`, `right`, and `parent` pointers)
   - The candidate explained a partial approach but couldn’t reach the optimal solution.

2. **Modified Merge Two Sorted Arrays Problem** → [Merge Sorted Array | LeetCode #88](https://leetcode.com/problems/merge-sorted-array/)

3. **Puzzle:**
   - _You have 10 bags, each containing 100 balls. Balls in 9 bags weigh 1 gram each, and balls in 1 bag weigh 2 grams each. Identify the defective bag using a digital weighing machine only once._  
     → [Defective Bag Puzzle](https://brainly.in/question/4745629)
   - **Follow-up:** What if **k** bags are defective?
   - The candidate needed several hints to reach the solution.

Unfortunately, the candidate was **rejected after this round**.

❌ **Outcome:** Not Selected

---

## ⚙️ Round 4 – Technical Interview 3

Almost all candidates who qualified for the third round were asked to:

- Revisit and solve **OA-style DSA questions**, and
- Answer **additional puzzle questions** to test logical reasoning and thinking approach.

This was a **short, interactive round** mainly used for final evaluation and consistency checking.

---

## 🌟 Overall Experience

The overall experience was **great and insightful**, especially since it was the candidate’s **first-ever interview**.  
Despite not being selected, the process helped in understanding how **interviewers evaluate both thought process and clarity**.  
The experience was **smooth, well-organized**, and served as **valuable learning** for future interviews.

---

## 💡 Advice for Others

> ⚡ **Practice DSA rigorously, at least complete the Striver Sheet by 2nd year and make good projects.**

Be prepared for **any question related to your resume**.  
**Don’t lie** about what you haven’t done, and always **explain your thought process** clearly during interviews.  
Consistency in preparation and honesty in communication make a big difference.
