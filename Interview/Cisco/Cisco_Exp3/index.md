## 🌟 Overall Experience

The Cisco on-campus process was an **intensive one-day virtual event** covering:

- Technical
- Managerial
- HR evaluation

Success depended on:

- Strong **Computer Networks & OS fundamentals**
- Deep understanding of **projects**
- Clear and confident **communication**

---

## 💡 Advice for Others

- **Computer Networks is very very important** — concepts like DNS, OSI layers, DHCP, FTP, and routing are asked in depth.
- **Know your projects very well:**  
  Be prepared to explain every design decision, edge case, scalability aspect, and deployment detail.
- Strengthen **Operating Systems fundamentals** alongside CN.
- Practice explaining concepts **clearly and confidently**.
- Think out loud during coding and discussions — interviewers value your thought process.
