# 💼 Interview Experience – Publicis Sapient (SDE Intern)

**Type:** On-Campus Opportunity  
**Role:** Associate Software Development Engineer (ASDE) Intern  
**Company:** Publicis Sapient

---

## 🧪 Round 1 – Online Assessment (OA)

The first round was an **online coding assessment**.

### 🔍 Round Details

- **Total Questions:** 3
- Difficulty level: **Easy to Medium**
- Questions were based on **standard DSA concepts**
- Required clear logic and proper implementation

### 📌 Performance

- Solved **2 out of 3 questions completely**
- Based on this performance, I was **shortlisted for the Technical Interview**

**References:**

- [Leetcode](https://leetcode.com/problemset/all/)
- [DSA - GFG](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/data-structures/)

## 💻 Round 2 – Technical Interview

This round focused on **core CS fundamentals** and **problem-solving skills**.  
The interviewer emphasized **clarity of thought and explanation** rather than tricky or advanced problems.

---

### 🧩 DSA / Problem Solving

- Asked an **easy Dynamic Programming question**: **House Robber**
- Interviewer asked me to:
  - Explain the **Top-Down (Memoization)** approach
  - Then explain the **Bottom-Up (Tabulation)** approach
- More focus was given to **how I think and explain**, not just the final solution

**References:**

- [Houser Robber - Leetcode](https://leetcode.com/problems/house-robber/)
- [Dynamic Programming - GFG](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/dynamic-programming/)

---

### 🧱 OOPS Concepts

Basic conceptual questions were asked, such as:

- What is **Encapsulation**
- What is **Polymorphism**
- General understanding of **OOPS principles**

Questions were straightforward and tested **fundamental clarity**.

**Reference:**

- [OOPS Concepts in Java - GFG](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/object-oriented-programming-oops-concept-in-java/)

## 💬 Round 3 – HR Interview

The final round was an **HR / Managerial interview**.

### 🔍 Questions Asked

- Introduce yourself
- What do you know about **Publicis Sapient**
- Why do you want to join this company
- Have you ever **led a team**
- How would you manage **different types of customers**
- General **behavioral and situational questions**

This round mainly evaluated:

- Communication skills
- Confidence and attitude
- Leadership and teamwork
- Understanding of the company

**References:**

- [Publicis Sapient](https://www.publicissapient.com/)
- [HR Interview Questions - Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/career-advice/interviewing/hr-interview-questions)

## 🌟 Overall Experience

The interview process was **smooth and well-organized**, though it was **time-taking** due to multiple rounds.  
Interviewers were **supportive** and focused more on:

- Fundamentals
- Clear explanation
- Problem-solving approach  
  rather than tricky or advanced questions.

---

## 💡 Advice for Others

- Focus strongly on **DSA basics**
- Be clear with **OOPS, OS, and CN fundamentals**
- Practice **explaining solutions out loud**
- Prepare basic **HR answers**
- Learn about the **company beforehand**
