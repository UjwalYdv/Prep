# 💼 Interview Experience – Optum TDP Intern

**Type:** On-Campus Opportunity  
**Role:** Technology Development Program (Intern)  
**Total Rounds:** 3 (Online Assessment + HR + Technical)

---

## 🧮 Round 0 – Online Assessment (Offline in CC Lab)

The assessment was conducted **offline** in the campus computer lab.  
It consisted of **MCQ-based questions** covering:

- Core **Computer Science fundamentals** (OOPs, DBMS, CN)
- **Aptitude** and **logical reasoning** questions
- Basic **C++ syntax and problem-solving**

The round was designed to test **conceptual understanding** and **clarity in basics**.  
It served as a shortlisting round for the HR interview.

✅ **Outcome:** Shortlisted for HR Round

---

## 🗣️ Round 1 – HR Interview (Duration: 10–15 minutes)

The first interaction was a **short HR discussion**, focused on communication, company understanding, and motivation.

Key question asked:

- “What do you know about Optum, and why do you want to join Optum?”

🧠 **Tip:** Before appearing, visit  
[Optum Official Site](https://www.optum.com/) and  
[Optum Careers](https://www.optum.com/careers.html)  
to understand their **vision, culture, and work in healthcare technology** — it helps you frame confident, genuine answers.

✅ **Outcome:** Cleared HR Round

---

## ⚙️ Round 2 – Technical Interview (Duration: 1 hour)

This round combined **technical and managerial discussions**.  
The interviewer started with a **coding problem in C++**, emphasizing **Object-Oriented Programming principles**.

I was asked to:

- Write a **C++ program** demonstrating OOPs concepts
- Explain **classes, inheritance, polymorphism**, and **encapsulation** while coding
- Discuss **real-world use cases** of OOP design
- Answer a few **managerial and behavioral questions** related to teamwork and decision-making

🧩 Useful Links to Revise Before the Interview:

- [OOPs Concepts in C++ – GeeksforGeeks](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/object-oriented-programming-in-cpp/)
- [C++ Interview Questions – GeeksforGeeks](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/cpp-interview-questions/)
- [Common HR & Managerial Questions – GeeksforGeeks](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/interview-experiences/managerial-interview-questions/)

✅ **Outcome:** Selected for Optum TDP Intern

---

## 🌟 Overall Experience

The overall experience was **very professional, smooth, and quick**.  
The interviewers were polite and focused on **concept clarity and communication** rather than tricky questions.  
Each round felt structured, and feedback was provided promptly.

---

## 💡 Advice for Others

> ⚡ **Your Resume is Your Syllabus.**

That means every project, skill, or technology you mention can be questioned, so **know your resume inside out**.  
Be clear about what you’ve done, why you did it, and what you learned.  
Confidence and honesty go a long way.
