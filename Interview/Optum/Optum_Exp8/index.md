# 💼 Interview Experience – Optum TDP Intern

**Type:** On-Campus Opportunity  
**Role:** Technology Development Program (Intern)  
**Total Rounds:** 2 (Technical + HR)

---

## 🧠 Key Takeaways from My Interview Experience

I would like to describe my internship interview experience through a few key points that may help others prepare better:

1️⃣ **Greet professionally, but warmly.**  
 A confident and polite greeting creates a positive first impression and sets the tone for the interview.

2️⃣ **Introduce yourself in line with the company’s values.**  
 Keep it short, authentic, and aligned with Optum’s focus on innovation and healthcare technology.

3️⃣ **Your Resume is Your Syllabus.**  
 Every single point mentioned in your resume can be questioned — so know it thoroughly.

4️⃣ **Expect questions from all sections of your resume.**  
 They start with general questions, then dive deep into your **projects**, especially focusing on **tools and technologies used**.

5️⃣ **Be prepared for basic DSA questions.**  
 Simple logic-based problems are asked to check your fundamentals.  
 📘 [Top Easy DSA Problems – LeetCode](https://leetcode.com/explore/interview/card/top-interview-questions-easy/)

6️⃣ **AI/ML is a trending focus area.**  
 Having at least one AI-ML-related project helps you stand out.  
 🔗 [Beginner ML Projects – GeeksforGeeks](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/machine-learning-projects/)

7️⃣ **Expect HR-style behavioral questions toward the end.**  
 They often relate to teamwork and leadership experiences reflected in your **Achievements section**.  
 📘 [Common HR Interview Questions – GeeksforGeeks](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/hr/hr-interview-questions/)

8️⃣ **Use “comeback questions” wisely.**  
 At the end, they usually allow you to ask questions. If you stumbled earlier, this is your moment to redeem yourself.  
 Ask **two professional** questions (like company goals or tech direction) and **one casual question** showing curiosity and enthusiasm for learning.

9️⃣ **Note:** This experience is specific to **Internship Interviews**, not final placements.

✅ **Outcome:** Selected for Optum TDP Intern

---

## 🌟 Overall Experience

The overall experience was **great** — the interviewers were friendly, professional, and genuinely curious about how I thought through problems.  
The entire process was well-organized and focused more on understanding my reasoning, learning attitude, and clarity than on perfection.

---

## 💡 Advice for Others

🍀 **Luck does matter — so collect some if you can!**

But along with luck, make sure your **communication, preparation, and mindset** are strong.  
Be honest, stay calm, and always show a willingness to learn. These qualities leave a lasting impression beyond just correct answers.
