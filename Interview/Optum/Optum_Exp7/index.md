# 💼 Interview Experience – Optum TDP Intern

**Type:** On-Campus Opportunity  
**Role:** Technology Development Program (Intern)  
**Total Rounds:** 2 (Web-Based Assessment + HR)

---

## 💻 Round 1 – Web-Based Assessment

The first round was a **web-based problem-solving test**, organized in a **tier-wise format**.  
I was able to solve up to **Tier 2** and secured a total score of **400 points**.

The test primarily focused on **logical reasoning**, **basic programming**, and **web-related problem-solving skills**.  
After the evaluation, I was shortlisted for the **HR interview round**.

✅ **Outcome:** Shortlisted for HR Round

---

## 🗣️ Round 2 – HR Interview

In the HR round, the interviewer mainly focused on **general and behavioral questions**, such as:

- “Why do you want to join Optum?”
- “What do you know about the company?”
- “How do you see yourself contributing to a corporate environment?”

The tone of the interview was polite and conversational.  
Despite a good interaction, I was not selected in the final shortlist.

❌ **Outcome:** Not Selected

---

## 🌟 Overall Experience

The overall experience was **quite good**.  
The process was smooth, professional, and gave me valuable insights into what recruiters look for in candidates.  
Even though I wasn’t selected, it was a great learning opportunity to understand real-world interview expectations.

---

## 💡 Advice for Others

✨ **Focus on your communication skills, especially in English.**

Confidence and clear expression make a strong impression, even more than technical perfection.  
Try to engage actively during HR discussions and speak fluently to convey your thoughts clearly.
